EQUABOILER Corporate Branding

In this project, I created the corporate visual identity of 2 brands of the company Calderos y Afines S.A., the institutional brand and the products brand. An extensive search for references was carried out, Isotypes and Logos were created, fonts and colors were chosen, all to form the first Corporate Visual Identity Manual of the brand with guidelines used in applications such as general stationery, uniforms, product branding and identity. digital. Photographs and videos were also generated that were part of the institutional video.
